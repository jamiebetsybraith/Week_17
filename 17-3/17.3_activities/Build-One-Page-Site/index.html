Flowers are in Bloom

Backyard gardening in Minnesota presents unique challenges and opportunities due to its diverse climate, with cold winters and warm summers. Tilling the soil in early spring helps prepare the garden beds by loosening the soil and mixing in organic matter, promoting healthy root growth for plants. For a vibrant and colorful garden, consider planting flowers like marigolds, cosmos, zinnias, and black-eyed Susans. These hardy blooms not only thrive in Minnesota's summer conditions but also attract beneficial pollinators, enhancing the beauty and health of your garden.
